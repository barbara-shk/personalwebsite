This is the story about a girl named Barbara

It me. I’m Barbara. And this is the story all about how my life got flipped, turned upside down and I'd like to take a minute just sit right there, I'll tell you how I became a pixel bender on the internet.

It was the year 2003, scrolling through the meanders of the world wide web as a moody pre-teen. Finding My People, a community of awkward kids who’d rather stay inside and make their internet space pretty.

Flash forward to 17 years of introducing and thinking of myself as a graphic designer and creative geek. It’s the dystopian year of 2020. The year where things changed for the world, myself included.

2020 Année 0

January

I started the year traveling on my own. On December 31st I left Paris a little before the clock hit midnight and by January 1st, I was in Rotterdam, Netherlands for five days. I was determined to make 2020 count, and solo traveling was my answer and how I usually recharge.

If you want more?

March

After months of self-analysis and a first dive into the real of personal development, CoVid-19 hit the worlwide charts. France fell under a strict lockdown and all my other travel plans went to sh*t. Thankfully, being an introvert and all, I managed to make the most use of this government-given time and started following several online courses, jumpstarting my formal web development education.

More?

September

So here we are, September 2020. The world is learning to cohabitate with global chaos and it’s time for me to go back to school and learn a new craft, which I also love. I’ve finally realised that what I truly love to do is to craft experiences and build things. I’m a people-pleaser at heart and if I can make my piece of the internet a better place, then let’s do this!

Moooore!
